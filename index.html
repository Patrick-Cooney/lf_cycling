FAQ

Do I need a fancy bike to join the team?

Absolutely not! You can participate with any bike that will allow you to ride your chosen distance. Though certain types of bikes will be more comfortable than others you're free to ride anything available to you.

How long is a typical ride?

With many of the rides on our list you can choose your own distance. They can be from as little as 19 miles to as much as 100 miles. We encourage you to choose whatever you're comfortable with.
